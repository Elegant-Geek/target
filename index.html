WELCOME

Target is a short System of a Down comic that takes place during the band's Toxicity era... but set in a mysterious apocalyptic wasteland. Based on an existing AU by gent1esatan. Enjoy!

ABOUT

Target (թիրախ) is short System of a Down comic I spent exactly one month on from start to finish (9.30.24-10.30.24). This timeline includes concept sketches of props and outfits, story development, plot and script revisions, drawing and inking, and launching this website. My previous comic series Third Eye (100+ pages) was released incrementally on Instagram, but to keep my sanity this time round and maintain my posting schedule, I decided to drop this comic in its entirety on 11.1.24 featuring all 40 pages across two posts. It is a suspenseful adventurous story that was inspired by fellow SOAD artist gent1esatan's original apocalyptic AU titled Toxicity. I was given permission to build a brand new story within this world and use its elements as inspiration.

The main conflict of this story is fueled by the grisly tasks within a series of deadly games. All players are in a race against time. Unlike Third Eye, this story is mostly plot-driven rather than character-driven. The lore for this universe is simple and thus the main premise and first draft were developed in a single day. The final draft of the first chapter sits at just over 10,000 words with a second chapter possibly in the works. Will I make more? Only time will tell. Enjoy the story!
