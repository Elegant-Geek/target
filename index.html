WELCOME

Target is a short System of a Down comic that takes place during the band's Toxicity era... but set in a mysterious apocalyptic wasteland. Based on an existing AU by gent1esatan. Enjoy!

ABOUT

Target (թիրախ) is short System of a Down comic I spent exactly one month on from start to finish (9.30.24-10.30.24). This timeline includes concept sketches of props and outfits, story development, plot and script revisions, drawing and inking, and launching this website. My previous comic series Third Eye (100+ pages) was released incrementally on Instagram, but to keep my sanity this time round and maintain my posting schedule, I decided to drop this comic in its entirety on 11.1.24 featuring all 40 pages across two posts. It is a suspenseful adventurous story that was inspired by fellow SOAD artist gent1esatan's original apocalyptic AU titled “Toxicity.” I was given permission to build a brand new story within this world and use its elements as inspiration.

The conflict of this story is mainly driven by the unspoken rules of some deadly game, secret keeping, and a simple race against time. Unlike Third Eye, the lore for this universe is simple and thus the main premise and first draft of the story was developed in a single day. The final draft of the first chapter sits at just over 10,000 words with a second chapter possibly in the works. Will I make more? Only time will tell. Enjoy the story!

MEET THE BAND

Serj Tankian:
Curious, introspective, and a little cautious. He prefers to experience things directly rather than observe and is always asking questions. He likes to unwind with tea and hot showers.

Daron Malakian:
Fiery, impulsive, and reactive. He wears his heart on his sleeve and is easily set off by upsetting or stressful events. He cares deeply for the wellbeing of Serj in particular.

Shavo Odadjian:
Caring, enthusiastic, and laidback. He prefers to avoid confrontation and provides some comic relief. He enjoys smoking weed and tends to go along with whatever's happening.

John Dolmayan:
The stubborn logical member of the group. He has no problem taking charge of situations or expressing his thoughts. He likes reading and napping despite his tough, hard-to-read exterior.
